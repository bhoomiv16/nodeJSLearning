Welcome To The Wayuu Market Wayuu Market is a window for Wayuu people to the world. We benefit the rural artisan communities through this online, small-producers market. Thousands of women from the deserts of La Guajira, weaving the patterns they dream, crafting the finest Colombian-woven bags. Each piece has a story to tell, and now, you can be a part of it!
